In this second message of our series, we explore Jesus' remarkable promise in Matthew 17:20 — that even the smallest, most sincere faith has the power to accomplish what seems impossible.

**Key Points:**
- The size of our faith matters less than the object of our faith — Jesus Himself.
- God-sized problems require God-centered faith, not self-sufficient striving.
- Mountain-moving faith is cultivated through prayer, Scripture, and community.
