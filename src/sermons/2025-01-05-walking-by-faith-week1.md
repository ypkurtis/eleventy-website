In this message, Pastor Dan Decker opens our new series on faith with a look at the famous words of 2 Corinthians 5:7 — *"For we walk by faith, not by sight."*

**Key Points:**
- Faith is not the absence of doubt, but the choice to trust God in the midst of it.
- Walking by faith means taking the next step even when we can't see the whole staircase.
- God is more interested in our trust than our understanding.
